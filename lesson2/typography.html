Typography

Heading Font: [Georgia, serif]

It is essential that our readers can read our website clearly and that is why I chose this type of font for the titles that make the page easy to read and that can bring a wonderful experience through reading and also through the page content.

Paragraph Font: [sans-serif, "Times New Roman", serif]

Helvetica is a good choice of typeface for reading that also gives the feeling of being easy to read. I want the reader to have a good experience within the website and to be able to read the characteristics of each part of the site very well, including the longer paragraphs.

Normal paragraph example

The company of any pet always evokes tenderness, company, and friendship, qualities that intensify when loneliness seeks to filter into people's lives; It is in these moments when the pet acquires a more important role, feeling it part of the family and even more, as a friend. Most people show a positive attitude through their relationship with a pet or companion animal, especially those who suffer from psychological or physical problems.


Colored paragraph example

For the ecological balance, animals play a determining role. They are leading actors in a large part of the phenomena and processes that meet the appropriate conditions for life. Even the tiniest animal on the planet plays a vital role in its ecosystem, so a change in its eating habits, that is, in food chains, can compromise the survival of everything around it.
