Audience Description

Honduras Weather provides information for all kinds of people interested in the weather, students, meteorologists, journalists, housewives, workers, lovers of the weather and its changes, etc. Our website is available for PC, mobile phones, tablets. in order to reach more people so that they can be informed daily of the weather forecasts.

Maria

Maria is a housewife, she is 32 years old, she lives in Tegucigalpa, Honduras, she has 2 beautiful children, every Saturday she enjoys outdoor activities with her husband, she visits our website every Friday and Saturday for weather information and prepares. to enjoy. time with your family.

Jose

José is an athlete, he is 23 years old and every morning he prepares for his training, he runs 3 kilometers a day, in order not to have difficulty he prepares by visiting our website to find out the weather, the forecasts on our website help him to enjoy a pleasant training without worry about the weather.

Scenarios

  1. Soccer players of the national team are in search of the least rainy place for their summer training.
  2. Hikers looking for the best place with the most stable weather to spend the Easter holidays.
  3. The cyclist and her friends go out for the weekend and look for information on the weather for Saturday.
  4. Primary school students conducting research on the climate in their locality.
  5. Journalists looking for information for the weather forecast.
